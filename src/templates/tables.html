NMR Spectroscopy

Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) spectroscopy is a technique used to determine the chemical structure and purity of compounds. It is based on the principle that certain atomic nuclei have magnetic properties.

IR Spectroscopy

Infrared (IR) spectroscopy is a technique used to analyze the chemical bonds present in a compound. It measures the absorption of infrared radiation by the sample, which is related to the vibrational modes of the chemical bonds.

Mass Spectroscopy

Mass Spectrometry is a technique used to identify the mass and structure of chemical compounds. It ionizes chemical species and sorts the ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio.
